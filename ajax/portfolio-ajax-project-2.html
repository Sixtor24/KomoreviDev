Project Description

24siete is a medical directory application that allows you to find and contact specialist doctors in Venezuela quickly and easily. With 24siete, you don't have to wait in long lines or depend on intermediaries to access quality medical care. You just have to enter the application, choose the type of consultation you need and see the profiles of the available doctors.

24siete has an extensive medical guide that covers various specialties, such as cardiology, dermatology, pediatrics, among others. Additionally, you can look for doctors based on signs or symptoms you have, such as headache, fever, cough, etc. This way, you can find the most suitable doctor for your case.
